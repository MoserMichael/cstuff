<br><br>Interesting discussion (in Russian) on the subject of climate change: it turns out that the present models would have predicted higher temperatures right now; This discrepancy has to be explained:&nbsp;some say that this might be due to reduced Solar activity and larger than usual volcano eruptions ; also some experts disagree on the models and on how much it should be warmer right now. Very complex subject that is.<br><br><a class="ot-anchor" href="http://avva.livejournal.com/2592843.html#comments" rel="nofollow">http://avva.livejournal.com/2592843.html#comments</a><br><br>Scientists had to warn us about the dangers of global warning; Now what is the right strategy? Nobody would have noticed a quiet approach; however with an alarmist key they had to brush aside doubts in our present day incomplete knowledge; this vigorous approach also has the danger of turning into an orthodoxy. Now the first victim of such a mode is climatology itself - false certainty and a lack of doubts does not really encourage productive inquiry.<br>All this is quite a complex issue.
